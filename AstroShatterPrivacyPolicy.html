We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. Since the Application does not collect any personal information, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

We strive to protect the integrity of the application local database; however, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page and will become effective immediately upon posting.
