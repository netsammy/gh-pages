Neon Bubble Shooter: Retro Pop Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Neon Bubble Shooter: Retro Pop, package name com.neon.bubbleshooter, handles information when you use the game. The game is published by Netsammy.

Information Stored on Your Device

The game stores gameplay progress and preferences locally on your device. This may include your high score, unlocked levels, level stars, sound and music settings, control mode, selected visual theme, and reset state. This local gameplay data is used only to provide game functionality and is not sent to our servers by the game.

Advertising

Neon Bubble Shooter: Retro Pop displays ads using Google AdMob. Google and its advertising partners may collect or receive information from your device, including advertising identifiers, app activity related to ads, device information, approximate location, diagnostics, and other data used to provide, measure, personalize, and improve ads.

Google processes advertising data according to its own policies. You can learn more at Google Privacy Policy and Google AdMob privacy guidance.

No Account or Login

The current version of the game does not require account creation, login credentials, Google Play Games sign-in, leaderboards, or achievements.

No In-App Purchases

The current version of the game does not offer in-app purchases or subscriptions.

How Information Is Used

  • Local gameplay data is used to save your game progress and preferences.
  • AdMob data is used by Google and its partners to provide, measure, personalize, and improve ads.
  • Diagnostics and performance data may be used to maintain reliability and prevent abuse.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Local gameplay progress is not shared by the game. Advertising-related data may be processed and shared by Google AdMob according to Google's advertising and privacy policies.

Data Deletion

You can delete local game progress from the Settings screen by using Reset Game Data. You can also remove local game data by uninstalling the app. Advertising data controlled by Google can be managed through your Google account and device ad settings.

Children's Privacy

Neon Bubble Shooter: Retro Pop is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can review and address the issue.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect app functionality and rely on Google SDKs for secure network communication related to ads. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, legal requirements, or data practices change. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
